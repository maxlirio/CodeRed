HOW TO PLAY

Goal — descend 15 floors. Kill the boss on floors 5, 10, and 15 to share your run.

Move — WASD / ←↑→↓. Bump enemies to attack. Space = wait.

Cast — Z X C V fire the spell in that slot. Aim spells let you click a tile. Hold Shift = charged (1.5× damage / cost).

Other — F = bow shot (if equipped). B = backpack / re-slot spells. 1–6 = use a relic. N = toggle AI narration. Esc = cancel aim / close menus.

Mobile — D-pad + action buttons below the canvas. Aim spells → tap the map tile to fire.
