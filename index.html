HOW TO PLAY

Goal

  • Descend all 15 floors.
  • Bosses wait on floors 5, 10, and 15.

Movement

  • W A S D or ← ↑ → ↓ — move one tile.
  • Walk into an enemy to melee them.
  • Space — wait a turn.

Spells

  • Z X C V — fire the spell in that slot.
  • Aim spells: click a tile to target.
  • Hold Shift (or tap CHARGED on mobile) for a 1.5× cast at 1.5× mana.

Weapons & items

  • F — bow shot (if you have a bow).
  • J — weapon enchant ability (after enchanting at the stand).
  • 1–6 — use a relic, potion, or sigil scroll from your bag.
  • B — open backpack to swap weapons or re-slot spells.

Town

  • Walk into any side of a shop to step inside.
  • Walk onto the vendor to buy. Step on the south rug to leave.

Other

  • + — hide/show the stats sidebar for a bigger view.
  • M — mute/unmute the music.
  • N — toggle AI enchants (Groq).
  • Esc — cancel aim or close any menu.

Mobile

  • D-pad + action buttons below the canvas.
  • Aim spells: tap the tile you want to hit.
